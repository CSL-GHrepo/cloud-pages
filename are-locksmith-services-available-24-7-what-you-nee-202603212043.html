Locksmith Pricing and Rates: What to Expect for 24/7 Service

Providing locksmith services outside normal business hours typically involves higher pricing due to the urgency and labor involved. Industry data suggests that after-hours locksmith services can cost 20% to 50% more compared to standard rates.

Factors influencing locksmith pricing include:

  • Time of day or night
  • Type and complexity of lock or key issue
  • Travel distance for locksmith arrival
  • Additional security hardware needed

For example, a basic residential lockout during daytime might cost $70-$120, while emergency assistance late at night could range from $120 to $200 or more. It's always advisable to request upfront estimates and confirm any possible surcharges before service begins.
