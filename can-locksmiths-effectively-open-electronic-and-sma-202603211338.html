Quick Overview: Why Electronic and Smart Locks Are Different

Electronic and smart locks operate differently than mechanical locks. Instead of physical keys, these systems rely on electronic signals, Bluetooth, WiFi, biometrics, or passcodes for access. Their increased security and convenience come with additional complexity, especially when it comes to unlocking during emergencies or lockouts.

For example, smart locks can integrate with home automation systems, remote monitoring, or smartphone apps, creating additional layers of digital lock security. This evolution has led to specialized locksmith smart lock services emerging in the industry.
