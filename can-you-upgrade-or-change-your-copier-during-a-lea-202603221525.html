Quick Answer: Can You Upgrade or Change Your Copier During the Lease Period?

Yes, upgrading or changing your leased copier during an active lease period is generally feasible, but it depends on your copier lease terms and the leasing company’s flexibility. Many commercial copier leasing agreements include options for copier lease modification or leasing contract adjustments to accommodate business copier upgrades without penalty. Understanding Copier Lease Terms and Their Impact on Upgrades

Copier lease agreements, by design, aim to lock a business into a contractual obligation for a set period, often 24 to 60 months. These contracts stipulate the copier model, monthly payments, maintenance terms, and early termination clauses. The ability to upgrade leased copier equipment mid-term hinges on these terms.

  • Fixed terms: Some agreements are rigid, barring equipment swaps or upgrades until lease expiration.
  • Upgrade clauses: Some contracts embed clauses allowing mid-term business copier upgrades, often with prorated charges or adjusted lease payments.
  • Early upgrade options: Certain vendors offer “early upgrade programs” incentivizing customers to switch to newer models partway through the lease.
  • Lease buyout and replacement: If upgrade clauses don’t exist, a buyout of the remaining lease can enable equipment change, but this often involves additional fees.

Industry studies indicate that over 60% of businesses opt for flexible copier lease agreements precisely for this reason: they anticipate evolving needs and want the option to upgrade or change equipment without hassle. 1

Options for Upgrading or Changing Copier Equipment During Your Lease

If your current lease agreement permits modifications or if you negotiate with your leasing provider, here are practical copier upgrade options:

  1. Lease Modification Addendums: Many leasing companies allow amendments to existing contracts that can upgrade the leased copier model or adjust specifications.
  2. Equipment Swap or Trade-In Programs: Vendors sometimes offer trade-in programs where you exchange your current leased copier for a newer model, resuming your lease on updated equipment.
  3. Early Termination and New Lease: If no upgrade option exists, businesses can terminate the lease early (often paying penalties) and enter a new lease agreement to get newer technology.
  4. Lease Extensions with Upgrades: You may extend your contract while increasing payments to cover enhanced copier capabilities.

For example, a medium-sized legal firm leased a basic multifunction copier, but with expansion, they needed high-volume color printing. Their leasing company negotiated a lease modification enabling an upgraded model upgrade with only a slight increase in their monthly payments. This is a prime example of lease agreement flexibility providing cost-effective copier upgrade options aligned with business growth.

Negotiating Lease Agreement Flexibility: Tips for Business Copier Upgrades

  • Negotiate upgrade paths upfront: Embed terms that facilitate switching equipment with minimal hassle or fees.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade my leased copier without terminating the lease?

Many leasing agreements allow for copier lease modification or amendments that enable upgrading leased copiers mid-term without terminating the contract. It depends on your lease terms and vendor policies. Always consult your leasing provider about upgrade options before considering termination.

What happens if I want to change copier equipment but have no upgrade clause?

Without an upgrade clause, changing copier equipment often requires lease buyout or early termination, which may involve substantial fees. Negotiating new lease terms or trade-in programs with your vendor could offer alternatives.

Are there additional costs when upgrading a leased copier?

Yes, upgrading leased copier equipment typically results in adjusted monthly payments or one-time fees depending on the new equipment’s value and lease length. Some vendors offer prorated payment adjustments to make upgrades affordable.

How can I ensure my leasing contract offers flexibility for business copier upgrades?

Before signing, request clauses permitting copier lease modifications and upgrading leased copier equipment. Also, inquire about early upgrade programs or trade-in options. Transparent, flexible contracts benefit growing businesses planning long-term copier needs.
